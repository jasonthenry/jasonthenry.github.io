## Projects

### Solar Panel Power Meter

A small power measurement system designed to measure voltage, current, and power from a solar panel. This project uses circuit analysis, voltage sensing, current sensing, signal conditioning, and embedded control to turn raw electrical measurements into useful output.

The goal is to build a working measurement tool, document the design process, and compare the system's performance under different lighting and load conditions.

**Skills used:** circuit design, voltage dividers, shunt resistors, op-amps, Arduino, LCD display, measurement, data collection, troubleshooting, technical documentation
